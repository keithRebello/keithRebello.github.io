Resume

I am a computer scientist whose research interests are in artificial intelligence and its intersection with cognitive science. More specifically, I am interested in understanding how artifcial intelligence can be used to better empathize with humans particularly through conversation. I want to create conversational agents that show high emotional intelligence and can better connect with people. I understand that is not an easy task but I am constantly on the lookout for novel discoveries in this field. I firmly believe in the potential of artifcial intelligence to be an effective tool for communication particularly in cases where conventional human communication maybe lacking such as with people who suffer with social and mental health issues.
I am currently pursuing my masters from Northeastern University and also work part time as a Research Assitant for the Relational Agents Group Lab.
